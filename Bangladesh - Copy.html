Bangladesh

Why Bangladesh?

Bangladesh, in a country of rich natural beauty including large mangrove swamps, mixing well-preserved beaches with mountain landscape. Bangladesh has a young population and developed a lot in terms of higher education opportunities, with higher education enrolments tripling since the 2000s, with over 2 million students enrolled in 2012. Hence, it offers a lot of learning opportunities and can help you become familiar with new technologies and give you opportunities to grasp new ideas and methods to comprehensive learning.
Studying an English-taught degree in Bangladesh will offer you better paid job opportunities in the country or abroad. Compared to other international study destinations, Bangladesh offers more affordable Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees. Bangladesh can be closer to your home country, so you will not have to spend a lot on travelling, and it can be easier to visit your family and friends during holidays.

Medical Education In Bangladesh

Bangladesh, being the ultimate destinations away from home for Nepalese medical students, provides a quality medical education to each and every new medical aspirant.
Colleges are under the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Health and family welfare(MOHFW) and are affiliated with a University on the respective region. Out of 96 recognized medical colleges in Bangladesh, 60 are private, 30 are public and 6 are run by the Bangladesh Armed forces.
All the medical colleges award the bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of surgery(MBBS) and/or Bachelor in Dental Surgery(BDS) degree. Most of the colleges are recognized by Nepal Medical Council(NMC), Medical Council of India(MCI) and are listed in WHO, world directory of medical studies.
